Franklin Breaks Record

Today was a record breaking day. Franklin has now gone 113 days without any rain, breaking the previous record set in 1985. This has caused problems around the area, including brush fires and a record breaking drought among others. Fire safety wranings have been issued around the area for taking the appropriate precautions.
